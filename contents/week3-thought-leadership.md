## Post 4 — LinkedIn (Thursday)

**Title**: The 6 Dimensions of AI Coding Capability — Where Are You Strong, and Where Are You Weak?

**Summary**:
Reference GitHub's SPACE framework (Satisfaction, Performance, Activity, Communication, Efficiency) for developer productivity, then argue that 2026 requires a new framework — one that measures AI *collaboration* capability, not just productivity. Deep-dive into NoMoreAISlop's 6-dimension analysis.

**Body Structure**:

1. **Opening**: GitHub's SPACE framework measured "developer productivity"
   - But in 2026, the question has shifted
   - It's no longer "how fast do you write code?"
   - It's "how well do you work WITH AI?"

2. **The 6-Dimension Framework — Explained**:

   | Dimension | What It Measures | Why It Matters |
   |---|---|---|
   | AI Collaboration Mastery | Quality of context you provide to AI | Input quality determines output quality |
   | Context Engineering | WRITE-SELECT-COMPRESS-ISOLATE strategies | The defining skill of 2026 (Karpathy) |
   | Burnout Risk | Work pattern sustainability indicators | Sustainable AI usage vs burnout spiral |
   | Tool Mastery | Effectiveness across different AI tools | Understanding tool-specific best practices |
   | AI Control Index | Strategic control vs vibe coding | Proactive usage vs passive acceptance |
   | Skill Resilience | Independent coding ability retention | Can you still build without AI? |

3. **Deep Dive: Skill Resilience** — the most critical dimension for the AI era
   - Case study: Luciano Nooijen (engineer at Companion Group) started a side project without AI tools and found himself struggling with tasks that used to be instinctive
   - "Things that used to be instinct became manual, sometimes even cumbersome"
   - Like athletes who never skip basic drills, developers need to regularly practice fundamentals
   - Stanford study: employment of 22-25 year old developers fell 20% between 2022-2025 — foundational skills matter more than ever

4. **How It Works**: NoMoreAISlop analyzes your actual Claude Code sessions across all 6 dimensions, providing scores, evidence quotes, and personalized growth recommendations
