Vibe Coding to Context Engineering to Agentic Engineering: Tracking Karpathy's Evolution of AI Coding

In just one year, Andrej Karpathy redefined how we talk about AI-assisted development three times. That progression tells us something important about where every developer needs to be heading.

February 2025: Vibe Coding.
Karpathy tweeted about "fully giving in to the vibes, embracing exponentials." The idea was simple: let AI handle the code, see what happens, iterate fast. It captured the excitement of a new era.

June 2025: Context Engineering.
The language shifted. Karpathy described it as "the delicate art and science of filling the context window with just the right information for the next step." This wasn't about clever prompts anymore. It was about designing what the model sees.

Early 2026: Agentic Engineering.
The latest evolution. Now the developer orchestrates autonomous agents who execute, while focusing on oversight and strategic direction.

What each stage really means:

Vibe Coding is "let AI handle it, see what happens." Great for prototypes. Risky for production. Most developers are still here.

Context Engineering is "design the information the model sees." This is the craft of effective AI collaboration. The best developers have moved here.

Agentic Engineering is "orchestrate autonomous agents, focus on oversight." This is the next frontier that's just emerging.

This progression mirrors every developer's growth path with AI. And it's why "AI collaboration capability" is becoming a core developer skill, not a nice-to-have.
