## Post 4 — LinkedIn (Thursday)

**Title**: Vibe Coding → Context Engineering → Agentic Engineering: Tracking Karpathy's Evolution of AI Coding

**Summary**:
Trace how Andrej Karpathy evolved his terminology from "Vibe Coding" (Feb 2025) → "Context Engineering" (June 2025) → "Agentic Engineering" (early 2026) within just one year, and interpret what this means for developers.

**Body Structure**:

1. **The Timeline**:
   - Feb 2025: Karpathy tweets "fully give in to the vibes, embrace exponentials" — Vibe Coding is born
   - June 2025: Karpathy shifts to "the delicate art and science of filling the context window with just the right information" — Context Engineering emerges
   - Early 2026: "You are orchestrating agents who do, and acting as oversight" — Agentic Engineering

2. **What Each Stage Means**:
   - Vibe Coding: "Let AI handle it, see what happens" — great for prototypes, risky for production
   - Context Engineering: "Design the information the model sees" — the craft of effective AI collaboration
   - Agentic Engineering: "Orchestrate autonomous agents, focus on oversight" — the next frontier

3. **The Insight**: This progression mirrors every developer's growth path with AI
   - Most are still at "vibe coding"
   - The best have moved to context engineering
   - The future demands agentic engineering skills
   - This is why "AI collaboration capability" is becoming a core developer skill
